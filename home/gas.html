Shells

Shells

The Shell gas station at 550 US-491 (also listed as 500 US Highway 491) is a convenient fuel stop strategically located right off Interstate 40 in Gallup, New Mexico. Operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, it caters to both local residents and cross-country travelers with a variety of fuel options including regular, midgrade, premium (V-Power NiTRO+), and diesel. The facility is equipped with a clean convenience store for snacks and road-trip supplies, an on-site ATM, an air pump for tires, and propane exchange services.
