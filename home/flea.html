Flea Market

The Gallup 9th Street Flea Market is one of the largest Native American markets in the United States, hosting over 500 vendors and attracting up to 10,000 visitors each week. It serves as a vibrant cultural hub where you can buy authentic handmade goods directly from Navajo, Zuni, and Hopi artisans.
