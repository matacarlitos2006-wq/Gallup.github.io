Public Transportation


There are 3 different types of Public Transportation when it comes to Gallup, New Mexico. There many different Public transporation for verities of transportation.


Gallup Express: provides local transportation in Gallup and Zuni, as well as intercity service between the two towns.


Navajo Transit: provides regional transportation within the Navajo Nation.


Greyhound: provides intercity transportation. The local Greyhound station is located at the Route 66 Mini Mart at 3060 West Highway 66.
