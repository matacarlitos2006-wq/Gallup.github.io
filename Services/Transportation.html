Zollinger library

Zollinger Library provides a variety of services and resources that support the University's instructional programs and users' needs for personal enrichment and recreation. Knowledgeable staff are available to organize library materials, to help users locate information, and to assist them in using print and online resources and computer equipment.

The library contains a computer lab, a conference room and group study rooms. In addition, the facility houses books, videos and periodical titles.
